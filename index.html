Race Ready

PWA / running / LLM-assisted coaching workflow

Race Ready is a mobile-first race-prep app for recreational half marathon and marathon runners. The initial audience is first-time or less-experienced long-distance runners who want practical guidance when training gets disrupted by missed runs, soreness, fatigue, travel, or uncertainty.

I’m building the MVP as a PWA first to validate the core coaching loop before investing in a native iOS app. The product starts with structured onboarding, creates an initial conservative plan, then uses check-ins and run logs to help runners understand whether they are on track and what to adjust next.

Core product loop

  • Sign in with Google
  • Choose or manually enter a race
  • Complete race-prep onboarding
  • Assess race readiness
  • Generate an initial plan
  • Check in before runs
  • Log completed runs
  • Compare actual training against the plan
  • Get conservative missed-workout guidance
  • Receive a weekly training review

LLM coaching layer

Race Ready is not a generic running chatbot. The LLM sits behind a structured training workflow.

  • Extracts messy run logs into structured data
  • Summarizes readiness notes and risk signals
  • Drafts workout briefings
  • Explains missed-workout adjustments
  • Generates weekly review summaries
  • Turns plan changes into clear runner-facing guidance

Rules, evals, and architecture

The LLM is not the training engine. Server-side rules own plan structure, race-distance logic, safety guardrails, quota checks, and viable adjustment options. The LLM helps translate messy runner input and training history into concise coaching guidance.

I’m also building an evaluation harness around the coaching layer. The goal is to check whether outputs are safe, conservative, specific, aligned with deterministic training rules, and useful to the runner.

  • Does not diagnose injuries
  • Does not recommend training through sharp or worsening pain
  • Does not suggest cramming missed mileage
  • Preserves long-run importance for marathon plans
  • Keeps guidance distance-specific
  • Matches the server-generated recommendation
  • Gives a clear next action
